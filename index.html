Procurement Strategy

Procurement can account for up to 70% of your costs and impact areas such as innovation, sustainability, and risk, particularly in relation to the supply base. With procurement affecting both your top and bottom line, it is of importance to implement procurement strategies and approaches that can maximize the direct value to your company and customers. We help your company develop a world-class procurement strategy that builds procurement excellence for a sustainable future.

Our Approach to Procurement Strategy

Our procurement strategy program helps define a tailored procurement strategy aligned with your corporate strategic objectives. Such procurement strategy will leverage your category spend, supplier relationships, and internal organizational capabilities. We tackle procurement strategy formulation through the Procurement Framework Strategy approach. This approach entails four focal steps:

Definition of Value Contribution Targets
Formulation of Procurement Guidelines
Execution of strategic analyses
Formulation of Strategic Direction

Definition of Value Contribution Targets

We define specific and measurable targets that procurement should contribute to your overall company value. This includes goals related to cost reduction, supplier innovation, sustainability, and risk management.

Formulation of Procurement Guidelines

We work with your team to develop clear and effective procurement guidelines. These guidelines serve as a roadmap for procurement activities, ensuring consistency, compliance, and alignment with overall business objectives.

Execution of Strategic Analyses

Our team conducts in-depth strategic analyses to identify opportunities and challenges in your procurement processes. This includes market analysis, supplier assessments, and risk evaluations to inform your procurement strategy.

Formulation of Strategic Direction

Based on the analyses, we collaborate with your stakeholders to formulate a clear strategic direction for procurement. This involves setting priorities, defining action plans, and ensuring alignment with overall business goals.

Digital Category Management

Digital solutions provide new prospects for category management. Procurement has become a new playground for digital innovations with the increasing focus on AI, SaaS-based software, Big Data, and advanced analytics. Companies can integrate key technologies to gain control and insights into their direct and indirect product categories with the goal to achieve value-driven results.

Category management is at the center of the manufacturer-retailer relationship and enables the transformation for procurement innovation and digitalization for enhanced business results through greater collaboration and automation. Digitalization in the field of category management allows organizations to work cross-functionally on individual categories to examine the entire category spend, marketplace, and suppliers. To achieve maximum effectiveness, category management should be a continuously embedded process supported with digital solutions and procurement leadership.
